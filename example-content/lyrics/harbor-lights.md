---
title: "Harbor Lights"
meaning: "A song about staying put when leaving would be easier — written after a winter spent deciding not to move away."
date: 2025-11-02
themes:
  - home
  - perseverance
---

Every ferry out is painted gold,
every map I own is creased and cold.
But the harbor lights stay on for me —
I was never meant for open sea.

So I'll learn the names of every street,
plant something green I'll never eat,
and call this stubborn little town
the only flag I won't take down.
