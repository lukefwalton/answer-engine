---
title: "Paper Crown"
meaning: "On the difference between being admired and being known."
date: 2026-03-08
themes:
  - identity
  - fame
---

They cut my name from magazines
and taped it to a paper crown.
It fits as long as I stand still;
it tears the moment I sit down.

So keep the throne, I'll keep the chair
beside the window, by the phone,
where someone calls me by the name
I had before the crown was known.
