title: "On Listening"
description: "Why attention, not opinion, is the first act of criticism."
date: 2025-09-14
themes:

---

Most of what passes for taste is impatience. We decide what a record is in the
first thirty seconds and spend the rest of the runtime collecting evidence for
the verdict. Listening — actually listening — means suspending the verdict long
enough to hear what the work is trying to do on its own terms.

The discipline I recommend is simple: before you say whether a thing is good,
be able to say what it is. Describe the arrangement. Name the choice the writer
made in the second verse. If you cannot describe it, you have not heard it, and
your opinion is about your mood, not the music.
