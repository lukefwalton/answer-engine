title: "Craft and Repetition"
description: "The case for doing the same small thing every day."
date: 2026-01-20
themes:

---

Nobody resents a carpenter for sharpening the same chisel every morning, but
writers treat routine as the enemy of inspiration. It isn't. Repetition is how
the hands learn what the mind cannot hold: a thousand small calibrations that
only show up as "voice" after years of showing up.

My rule is one page before noon, no exceptions and no judgment. The page is
allowed to be bad. The streak is not allowed to break. Inspiration, when it
comes, finds the workshop already open.
