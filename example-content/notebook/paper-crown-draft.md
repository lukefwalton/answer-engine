Private working notes. The first draft had a second bridge naming the
magazine that ran the profile — cut it because the song is about the
crown, not the people who made it, and naming them would have made it a
grudge. The "chair by the window" image came from a phone call with my
sister; that stays between us, the song only keeps the chair.
